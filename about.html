About Division Auto Body Inc

Division Auto Body Inc in Keyport, NJ has served the community since 1977 as a trusted collision and auto body repair shop. With nearly 50 years of experience, NJ State License #03175A, and a strong reputation across Monmouth and Middlesex Counties, the shop continues to deliver high-end, factory-level repair standards.

Facility & Capabilities

Our 3,300 sq. ft. facility on Route 35 features 6 service bays and a dedicated paint booth, giving us the capacity to handle daily drivers, luxury vehicles, exotic cars, and commercial fleets with precision and efficiency.

Ownership & Direction

Under the ownership of Denis Denisov, the business remains focused on quality work, transparent service, and a long-term reputation that can continue to stand strong well into the future.

Our Services

At Division Auto Body Inc in Keyport, NJ, we offer full‑service collision and auto body repair for everyday drivers, luxury vehicles, and commercial fleets. Our 3,300 sq. ft. facility, 6 service bays, and dedicated paint booth give us the capacity to handle everything from small dings to full‑side collision work with precision and factory‑level standards.

  • Collision Repair – Comprehensive accident and structural repair with insurance‑level documentation.
  • Paintless Dent Repair – Preserve factory paint whenever possible with precise dent removal.
  • Scratch Removal – Eliminate surface scratches and restore your finish seamlessly.
  • Car Bumper Repair – Repair and refinish damaged bumpers with accurate color matching.
  • Panel Replacement – Replace damaged panels with precise fit and factory‑grade alignment.
  • Frame Straightening – Restore structural integrity after significant impact.
  • Aluminum Repair – Specialized work on modern aluminum‑bodied vehicles.
  • Rust Repair – Cut out compromised metal and seal the structure for long‑term protection.
  • Hail Damage Repair – Remove hail dents with paintless techniques and full refinishing when needed.
  • Upholstery Repair – Restore seats, door panels, and interior trim.
  • Custom Auto Body Work – Styling, minor modifications, and custom restorations.
  • Fleet Services – Collision repair, dent removal, and cosmetic work for commercial, rideshare, and business fleets.

Insurance & Total Loss Support

We work with all major insurance companies and provide full support throughout the claim process. If your vehicle has been declared a total loss, we help you understand your options and validate the insurer’s appraisal to ensure a fair outcome.

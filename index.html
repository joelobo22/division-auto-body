Our Legacy in Keyport

Division Auto Body Inc in Keyport, NJ has been serving the community as a trusted collision and auto body repair shop since 1977. With nearly 50 years of experience, NJ State License #03175A, and a 3,300 sq. ft. facility on Route 35, we’ve become a cornerstone of Keyport and the surrounding Monmouth and Middlesex County towns.

Under the ownership of Denis Denisov, the shop continues to uphold the same standards of quality, transparency, and customer‑centric service that have defined Division Auto Body for decades.
